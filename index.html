Making a statement in

Downtown Greenville

Located at 55, 61, and 75 Beattie Place in vibrant Downtown Greenville, South Carolina, these attractive glass curtain wall buildings are conveniently positioned less than one half mile to the terminus of I-385. One and Two Liberty Square were constructed in 1983 and 1986, respectively, and were extensively renovated in 2005. The One Liberty Square passenger elevator cabs were recently renovated in addition to a new full service Fitness Center to be completed in 2013.

Tenants

Notable tenants in One and Two Liberty Square include: Sherman Financial Group; Brown Mackie College; the U.S. Attorney's Office; Gallivan, White & Boyd; Commerce Club; Ernst & Young; Dority & Manning; and Robert Half International.
